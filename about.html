About Me

My Story

Hi, I am Ana Roberts a UX/UI designer with a background in industrial/product design, currently with a master's in design by creative practice, I specialise in producing efficient, aesthetically friendly products and services. In my last 10 years, I have been working for the toy and fashion industry, delivering successful and recognised results to customers and businesses around the world and to retailers like: Walmart, Tesco, Hamleys, Morrisons, Argos, Aldi, B&M etc... All my projects are based on researching and analysing behaviours, needs, struggles and pain points of people/market, to create satisfying, functional, aesthetically friendly and innovative solutions that bring better experiences to their everyday living.
