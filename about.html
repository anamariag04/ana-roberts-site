About me

Hi, I am Ana Maria Roberts a UX/UI designer with strong background in industrial/product design, branding, licensing, and 3D design; with knowledge in HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

In my last 10 years, I have designed and lead projects for the children and fashion industry, delivering over hundred of thousands products/services to customers and businesses around the world.

All my projects are based on research and analysis of behaviours, needs, struggles and pain points of people. Also, in understanding with empathy their interests for me to create satisfying, functional, aesthetically friendly and innovative solutions that deliver better experiences to their everyday living.

Design is not only my profession, but also my passion to create and improve towards more accessible experiences for people and help businesses to expand.
